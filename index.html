Spacey Latte's Cocoa Matcha Cookie Recipe:

    1. Melt a stick of butter until soft or liquid (both), add one large egg, cup of white sugar, brown sugar, and teaspoon of baking powder.
    2. Mix. 3. Add 1 1/4 cup of flour, toss in cocoa and stir so that the mix is brown but not too dark. Now add matcha so that it comes out a dark green. add in chocolate chips, semi or dark preferrable.
    4. Put in oven at 350 degrees for about 10-12 minutes.

    Let cool before eating.
    Should soft and gooey, or lightly crisp on the outside, gooey on the inside.
